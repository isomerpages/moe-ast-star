<p>Teacher-leadership programmes are designed to guide teacher-leaders in
championing good music pedagogies and leading other teachers.</p>
<h3>Senior / Lead Teacher Programme</h3>
<p><strong>FOR</strong>: Senior Teachers
<br><strong>PERIOD</strong>: Part-time basis over the course of 6 months</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/starseniorlead.jpg">
</div>
<p>Senior Teachers are skilful and experienced teachers who serve as mentors
and role models to the teachers in their schools, sharing with them their
teaching expertise and pedagogical knowledge.</p>
<p>Complementing the AST's TLP 1 and 2, the STAR Senior/Lead Teacher (ST/LT)
Programme advocates for subject disciplinarity teacher leadership. Nominated
by their School Leaders, this 6-months part time uses a work-reflect-work
model:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Nurtures a collaborative and reflective culture among Art/Music Senior
and Lead Teachers,</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develops their competencies as pedagogical leaders, and;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Revisits their personal arts teaching beliefs and practices as part of
professional renewal, reinvention and positive transformation</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Senior Teachers also engage in reflective practice via completion of a
personal reflection journal and participation in group discussions and
presentations.</p>
<p>Core learning areas are as follows:
<br><strong>Module One:</strong>&nbsp;Pedagogical Mastery for 21st CC Arts
Educators
<br>Arts Pedagogical Workshop</p>
<p><strong>Module Two:</strong>&nbsp;Situated Learning
<br>Overseas Learning Journey
<br>Industry Exposure&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Module Three:</strong>&nbsp;Pedagogy in Practice
<br>Classroom Practice</p>
<p></p>
