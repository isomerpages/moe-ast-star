<p>This edition of STAR-Post (Art) explores how art teachers can sustain
their passion for teaching and personal artmaking. It highlights reflections
on balancing professional demands with artistic practice, practical strategies
like mindful drawing, and the importance of keeping creativity alive to
inspire students. Through stories, sketchbooks, and teacher profiles, the
issue celebrates the unique role of art educators and encourages nurturing
both artist and teacher within to stay inspired and impactful.</p>
