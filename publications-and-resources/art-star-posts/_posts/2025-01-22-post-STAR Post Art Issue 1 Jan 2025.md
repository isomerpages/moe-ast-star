<p>This edition of STAR-Post is brimming with inspiring ideas from our educators
to ignite both hearts and minds! Hear from Larry (SEAB), Jacelyn (AEB),
and Clifford (STAR) as they reflect on the transformative journey of art
education. Dive into “Teachers’ Voices” to explore heartfelt stories and
innovative practices shared by our colleagues. Plus, discover personal
aspirations for art education in Singapore, emphasizing the power of art-making
and meaningful conversations in shaping students’ lives.</p>
